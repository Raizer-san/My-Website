How to play Roblox?
To play the game, first you have to make a Roblox Account.
(Click on the image below to redirect you to the Official Roblox Website.)
Once you have created your account. Head to the search bar and search for the keywords that you want to play, or click “discover” (on the top-right of the screen), to browse the available games (also called experience). Once you have found the experience that you want to play, click on it and press the green play icon to launch the experience. If you haven’t downloaded the roblox application yet, you will need to download it first. Once you have downloaded it, click the green play icon again and there will be a pop-up questioning you to open Roblox, click “open roblox” then enjoy the experience.
